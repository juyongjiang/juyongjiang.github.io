Biography

Juyong Jiang received his Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology, Hohai University with honors of the Excellent Bachelor Thesis Award and recommended as the Outstanding Bachelor Thesis in Jiangsu Province in July. 2020. He used to be a member of Mathematical Modeling Lab and then joined AI Development Group, under the supervision of Prof. Jianjun Ni. In August 2019, he got the PhD offer from College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University. But since he wanted to pursue his interest in the field of Computer Vision, he gave up this precious opportunity eventually. To do better research in the future, from Oct. 2019 to Oct. 2020, he was a Research Intern in Image & Video Analysis Group (IVA), National laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academic of Sciences (CASIA), supervised by Prof. Jing Liu. But now he is a Vision Algorithm Intern in Smart More Co., Ltd. , supervised by Dr. Nianjuan Jiang.

My researches aim at endowing machines with the capability to perceive, understand, and reconstruct the real world with the following focuses: (1) developing deep learning algorithms that endow machines and devices to solve complex tasks with larger autonomy; (2) enabling applications in Machine Learning and Computer Vision, mainly working on: Object Detection, Instance Segmentation, Visual Self-supervised Learning, and Visual Transformer.

Note: I am looking for PhD and RA positions. If you have any interests, feel free to contact me.
