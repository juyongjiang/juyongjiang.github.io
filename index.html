Biography

Juyong Jiang received his Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology, Hohai University with honors of the Excellent Bachelor Thesis Award and recommended as the Outstanding Bachelor Thesis in Jiangsu Province in July. 2020. He used to be a member of Mathematical Modeling Lab and then joined AI Development Group, under the supervision of Prof. Jianjun Ni. From Oct. 2019 till present, he is a Research Intern in Image & Video Analysis Group (IVA), National laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academic of Sciences (CASIA), supervised by Prof. Jing Liu.

My researches aim at endowing machines with the capability to perceive, understand, and reconstruct the real world with the following focuses: (1) developing deep learning algorithms that endow machines and devices to solve complex tasks with larger autonomy; (2) enabling applications in Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing, such as Object Detection, Instance Segmentation, Scene Text Detection&Recognition, Graph Convolution Network.
